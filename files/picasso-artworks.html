Description

Pablo Diego Jos Francisco de Paula Juan Nepomuceno Mara de los Remedios Cipriano de la Santsima Trinidad Ruiz y Picasso was a Spanish painter, sculptor, printmaker, ceramicist, and theatre designer who spent most of his adult life in France. Wikipedia
Born: October 25, 1881, Mlaga, Spain
Died: April 8, 1973 (age 91years), Mougins, France
Periods: Cubism, Modern art, Picasso's Blue Period, Surrealism See more
Influenced by: Vincent van Gogh, Paul Czanne See more
Children: Paloma Picasso, Paulo Picasso, Claude Pierre Pablo Picasso, Maya Widmaier-Picasso
Spouse: Jacqueline Roque (m. 19611973), Olga Khokhlova (m. 19181955)
Full name: Pablo Diego Jos Francisco de Paula Juan Nepomuceno Mara de los Remedios Cipriano de la Santsima Trinidad Ruiz y Picasso
